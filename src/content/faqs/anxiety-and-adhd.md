question: "Can swimming help kids with anxiety or ADHD?"
category: "general"
surfaces:
  - "faq"

---

Many families find it does. Research shows regular exercise can improve attention, emotional regulation, and anxiety in kids — and swimming's rhythmic breathing, structure, and measurable progress make it especially well suited. It's not a substitute for professional care, but it can be a powerful complement. Read more about [swimming and well-being](/our-approach/#well-being) on our Approach page.
