Our deck practices follow the **Minor Athlete Abuse Prevention Policy (MAAPP)** — including two-coach rules for one-on-one interactions and required reporting protocols. Lifeguard, CPR, and AED certifications are maintained for staff on deck. If you ever have a concern, talk to Coach Robbie directly.
