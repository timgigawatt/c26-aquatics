Team membership is billed monthly and includes practice time, coaching, and onsite functional strength, plyometrics, and injury-prevention training. There's a **$100 annual placement fee** per swimmer; meet registration and entry fees are billed separately as they come up. There are no mandatory fundraising fees or parent hours. Parent volunteer requirements will be released at a later date. Current pricing for each training group is listed on the **Team** page.
